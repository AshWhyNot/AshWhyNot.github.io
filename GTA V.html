A computer action game from the Grand Theft Auto series.
released on PlayStation 3 and Xbox 360 consoles on September 17, 2013 by Rockstar Games studio.
The action was set in the fictional city of Los Santos and in non-urban areas
in the special Blaine County in the state of San Andreas,
created based on the city of Los Angeles and California.

Hello in Los Santos!

When a ->young thug<-, a ->retired thief<- and a ->terrifying psychopath<- become entangled
with the most terrifying and unstable members of the world of crime,
the US government and the entertainment industry, they must pull off a series of
dangerous heists to survive in a ruthless world where they can trust no one,
especially each other.

Get a taste of GTA Online, a dynamic and constantly evolving world of online fun
for up to 30 players, where you can transform from a street brawler
into the ruler of a criminal empire.

Players can enjoy top-of-the-range vehicle upgrades and additions like
a faster career start, as well as GTA Online gameplay improvements,
updates and content released since launch that can be enjoyed alone or with friends.

Perform daring cooperative heists, join adrenaline-packed stunt races, compete in unique
adversary modes, or simply relax in social spaces including: nightclubs, arcades,
party penthouses, car rallies and more.
